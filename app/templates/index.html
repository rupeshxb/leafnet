About the diseases

🌱

Healthy

No visible signs of disease. Leaf texture and colour are consistent with a well-growing plant. Regular monitoring is advised.

🍂

Early Blight

Caused by Alternaria solani. Presents as dark, concentric-ring spots on older leaves. Manageable with timely fungicide application.

☁️

Late Blight

Caused by Phytophthora infestans. Spreads rapidly under cool, humid conditions. Can devastate a crop within days if untreated.
